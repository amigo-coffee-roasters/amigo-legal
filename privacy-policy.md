# Privacy Policy

**Amigo Roasters LLC — Payment Receipt Automation**

Last Updated: May 2026

## Overview

This application is developed and operated exclusively by Amigo Roasters LLC 
for internal business automation purposes. It is not distributed to or used 
by any third parties outside of Amigo Roasters LLC.

## Data Collected

This application accesses QuickBooks Online data solely to automate internal 
payment receipt processing workflows. Specifically, the application reads 
invoice data and posts payment records on behalf of Amigo Roasters LLC.

## Data Storage

Payment data processed by this application is stored in Microsoft Dataverse 
within the Amigo Roasters LLC Microsoft 365 tenant. This data is accessible 
only to authorized Amigo Roasters LLC staff.

## Data Sharing

Data accessed via QuickBooks Online is not shared with, sold to, or disclosed 
to any third parties. It is used exclusively for internal accounting automation.
